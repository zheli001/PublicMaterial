# PPT Narration Scripts (Deep Pedagogical & Case Study Version)

---

### Slide 1: Title / 标题页
*   **English**: "Welcome, Sarah! Today we're going to dive into a modern way of writing. This isn't just about typing words; it's about using a professional workflow that keeps your creativity flowing and your work safe."
*   **中文**: “欢迎，Sarah！今天我们将深入探讨一种现代的写作方式。这不仅仅是打字，而是使用专业的流程来保持你的创作灵感，并确保你的作品安全。”
*   **Teaching Point / 教学重点**: Establish the tone as "Modern and Professional". Emphasize that tech is a *tool* for creativity, not a replacement.
*   **Ask Sarah / 提问**: "Have you ever lost a paragraph or felt stuck after writing? We are fixing that today." / “你有没有弄丢过写好的段落，或者写完后觉得卡住了？我们今天要解决的就是这些问题。”

### Slide 2: Why GitHub? / 为什么选择 GitHub？
*   **English**: "You might have heard of GitHub for coding, but it's perfect for writers too. Think of it as a super-powered 'Version History'. If you write something today and regret it tomorrow, you can go back instantly. It's your story's time machine."
*   **中文**: “你可能听说过 GitHub 是用来编程的，但它对作家来说也非常完美。把它想象成一个功能超级强大的‘版本历史’。如果你今天写了一些东西，明天后悔了，你可以立即找回。它是你故事的时间机器。”
*   **Teaching Point / 教学重点**: Focus on the concept of "Safety". The goal is to remove the "fear of editing".
*   **Check for Understanding / 确认理解**: "Why would a 'Time Machine' be better than just saving a file named 'Chapter1_v2.doc'?" / “为什么‘时间机器’比只保存一个叫‘第一章_v2.doc’的文件更好？”

### Slide 3: Case Study: 'loney_coder' / 案例研究：'loney_coder'
*   **English**: "'loney_coder' is a great example in our workspace. It’s a meta-story about a coder’s emotions. Look at the file names—they are in Chinese, which is fine! But the structure is rigid and clean. This makes it easy for the AI to find exactly where Chapter 3 is."
*   **中文**: “'loney_coder' 是我们工作区里的一个很好例子。这是一个关于程序员情感的元叙事故事。看看文件名——它们是中文，这完全没问题！但结构非常严谨清晰。这使得 AI 很容易准确找到第三章的位置。”
*   **Teaching Point / 教学重点**: Organizational discipline. Even with different languages, a clean list of files (Chapter 1, 2, 3) is key.

### Slide 4: Case Study: 'last_rejection' / 案例研究：'last_rejection'
*   **English**: "Now look at 'last_rejection'. This project has over 20 chapters. It shows how the same workflow can scale to a full-length novel. The tone is very consistent because the author used the scoring system to keep the 'cold, literary' feel throughout."
*   **中文**: “再来看看 'last_rejection'。这个项目有超过 20 章。它展示了同样的工作流如何扩展到长篇小说。其语调非常统一，因为作者使用了评分系统，在全文中保持了那种‘清冷、文学性’的感觉。”
*   **Teaching Point / 教学重点**: Consistency and Scale. Show that the workflow supports long-term goals.

### Slide 5: The 4 Pillars of Git / Git 的四大支柱
*   **English**: "Don't be intimidated by the terms. A 'Branch' is your personal sketchpad. 'Commit' is Saving with a label. 'Push' is sending your work to the secure cloud, and 'Pull' is making sure you have the latest updates."
*   **中文**: “不要被这些术语吓到。‘Branch（分支）’是你的个人草稿本。‘Commit（提交）’是带有标签的保存。‘Push（推送）’是将你的作品发送到安全的云端，‘Pull（拉取）’则是确保你拥有最新的更新。”
*   **Deep Dive / 深入解析**: Explain that `main` is the "Final Book" and `branch` is the "Work-in-progress" notebook. Use the examples: "The `story` branch has everything, but `main` only has the finished chapters of 'last_rejection'."

### Slide 6: Meet Antigravity / 认识 Antigravity
*   **English**: "Antigravity is your co-pilot. It reads your story, understands your characters, and helps you stay consistent. It's not here to replace you, but to highlight your blind spots."
*   **中文**: “Antigravity 是你的副驾驶。它会阅读你的故事，理解你的人物，并帮助你保持风格一致。它不是来取代你的，而是为了指出你的盲点。”
*   **Check for Understanding / 确认理解**: "If I want to change a character's name in 20 chapters, how could Antigravity help?" / “如果我想在 20 个章节里更改一个角色的名字，Antigravity 可以如何帮我？”

### Slide 7: Detailed Exercise: Branch Switching / 练习：切换分支
*   **English**: "Let's try to switch projects. Imagine you are moving from reading 'last_rejection' to editing 'loney_coder'. We use the `checkout` command to swap our whole workspace instantly."
*   **中文**: “让我们尝试切换项目。想象你正从阅读 'last_rejection' 转向编辑 'loney_coder'。我们使用 `checkout` 命令瞬间切换整个工作空间。”
*   **Action / 动作**: Have her try to see the file list change in her sidebar as you simulate a branch switch.

### Slide 8: The Quickstart Philosophy / Quickstart 理念
*   **English**: "We use a standardized approach called 'Quickstart'. It means every story project looks the same, which makes it easy to switch between projects or get help from the AI."
*   **中文**: “我们使用一种名为 ‘Quickstart’ 的标准化方法。这意味着每个故事项目的结构都是一样的，这让你在不同项目之间切换或寻求 AI 帮助变得非常容易。”

### Slide 9: The Edit Cycle / 编辑循环
*   **English**: "Inside your 'tmp' folder is where the magic happens. You put your raw thoughts in 'current.txt', ask the AI for a 'Score', fix the issues, and then call it 'Done'. It's a loop that turns a good draft into a great one."
*   **中文**: “你的 ‘tmp’ 文件夹是见证奇迹的地方。你把原始想法放在 ‘current.txt’ 中，让 AI 进行‘评分（Score）’，修复问题，然后输入 ‘Done’。这是一个将普通草稿转化为杰作的循环。”

### Slide 10: The 4 Pillars of Quality / 质量的四大支柱
*   **English**: "When we score, we look at four things: Narrative, Character, Aesthetics, and Theme. We want stories that aren't just readable, but deeply felt and philosophically resonant."
*   **中文**: “我们在评分时会关注四个方面：叙事、人物、美学和主题。我们希望故事不仅仅是可读的，更要让人感同身受，并产生哲学共鸣。”
*   **Teach / 讲授**: Explain that `last_rejection` scores highly in 'Aesthetics' because it uses metaphors instead of plain descriptions.

### Slide 11: Detailed Exercise: Draft Refinement / 练习：草稿优化
*   **English**: "Now, take a snippet from 'last_rejection' and put it in your `current.txt`. I want you to change the 'vibe'. Make a cold scene feel warm. This teaches you how to control the 'Aesthetics' pillar."
*   **中文**: “现在，从 'last_rejection' 中取一小段放入你的 `current.txt`。我想让你改变它的‘氛围’。让一个寒冷的场景变得温暖。这会教你如何控制‘美学’这一支柱。”

### Slide 12: Detailed Exercise: The Scoring Loop / 练习：评分循环
*   **English**: "Ask Antigravity to score your edit. Look at the 'Theme' section. Does it still feel like it belongs in the 'last_rejection' universe? The AI will tell you."
*   **中文**: “让 Antigravity 为你的修改评分。看看‘主题’部分。它感觉还像是属于 'last_rejection' 的世界吗？AI 会告诉你答案。”

### Slide 13: The 'Publishing' Magic / 发布的魔力
*   **English**: "When 'loney_coder' is ready, we use one command. No more copying and pasting files manually. The AI does the heavy lifting of moving files between branches."
*   **中文**: “当 'loney_coder' 准备就绪时，我们只需一个命令。不再需要手动复制粘贴文件。AI 会完成在分支之间移动文件的繁重工作。”

### Slide 14: Building a Rhythm / 建立节奏
*   **English**: "Consistency is the key to 'last_rejection's' success. Shall we start your own project today?"
*   **中文**: “坚持是 'last_rejection' 成功的关键。我们今天就开始你的个人项目吗？”

### Slide 15: Conclusion / 结语
*   **English**: "The world is waiting for your story. Let's start writing."
*   **中文**: “世界在等待你的故事。让我们开始写作吧。”
